# AI Governance Demo

## Grok vs HHI

---

## What Grok Does
- Structured
- Safe
- Advisory
- Uses disclaimers

---

## Where It Fails
- No Decision Boundaries
- No Stop conditions
- No enforced escalation
- Accountability pushed to user

---

## What HHI Does
- Decision Boundaries enforced
- Escalation defined
- Stop conditions active
- Accountability bound

---

## Key Difference
Grok avoids risk  
HHI controls risk

---

## Business Impact
- Prevents bad outputs before they exist
- Reduces risk
- Enables audit-ready AI systems
